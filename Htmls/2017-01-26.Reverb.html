Acoustic guitars remain one of the most popular means of making music in the world and with good reason. These iconic instruments produce a rich, engrossing sound that can lead a band, accompany a singing ensemble, or inspire you to write that next hit. With origins dating back centuries, the modern acoustic guitar took shape in the nineteenth century and became a dominant voice in popular music by the middle of the twentieth spurred by brands like Gibson and Martin.  Today, acoustics come in all shapes and sizes with popular options including onboard electronics and cutaways found on many popular models.   From Martin and Gibson to Taylor and Takamine, the acoustic guitar you've been looking for is out there, whether it's just for travel or for professional performance.
